    Intro

    This addon adds a new mana bar into your game along with 2 staffs. You can connect your own addon into this addon by connecting to the correct scripting event. The staffs can be crafted in survival. To connect this addon to yours, you will need to connect to `hanprogramer:send_mana_update`. Check the files to see exactly how.

    Crafting Recipes

    Item Description:

    The ender staff allows you to teleport few blocks ahead of you. Costs 3 mana

    The crimson staff allows you to regenerate your health and mob around 3 radius of blocks. Costs 7 mana.
