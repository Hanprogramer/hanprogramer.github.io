    Tumble Craft : Donuts Update

    This addons add in a new series of foods, specifically donuts! Each of them has unique effects and crafting recipes. Donuts are stackable in 6 items per stack. Making them quite big, but, this item will definitely help you along your survival journey

     

    This addon require minimum minecraft 1.16 as the version

    This addons add in a new series of foods, specifically donuts! Each of them has unique effects and crafting recipes. Donuts are stackable in 6 items per stack. Making them quite big, but, this item will definitely help you along your survival journey.

    This addon is survival friendly, and multiplayer firendly. You can use it on your realms, servers, or multiplayer world. Besides that, this addon also add in porkchop sword and pumpkin sword (only for decoration purposes for now, also you can eat and cook the porkchop sword xD, this idea submited by GaloreInfernoLion and PlagueVamp).
